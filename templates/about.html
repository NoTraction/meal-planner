About Meal Planner

What We Do

Meal Planner is your personal assistant for healthy eating and efficient meal management. Our platform helps you:

  • Set up your dietary preferences and health goals
  • Get personalized meal plans based on your profile
  • Track your daily calories and weekly meal progress
  • Generate shopping lists for your planned meals
  • Discover recommended videos and resources for healthy living

Whether you want to cut, bulk, or simply eat healthier, Meal Planner makes it easy to stay on track and reach your goals. All your data is private and used only to improve your meal planning experience.
